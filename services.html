Our Financial Services

Mutual Funds

Mutual funds are investment vehicles that pool money from multiple investors to invest in a diversified portfolio of stocks, bonds, and other securities. At Rudra Capital, we help you select the right mutual funds based on your risk profile and financial goals.

  • Professional management by experienced fund managers
  • Diversification to reduce risk
  • Accessibility with small investment amounts
  • Liquidity - easy to buy and sell
  • Transparency in operations

Systematic Investment Plan (SIP)

A Systematic Investment Plan (SIP) is a disciplined way of investing in mutual funds. It allows you to invest a fixed amount regularly at predefined intervals, helping you build wealth over time through the power of compounding.

  • Disciplined investing approach
  • Averages out investment cost through rupee cost averaging
  • Power of compounding for wealth creation
  • Flexible investment amounts
  • No need to time the market

Child Education Planning

Planning for your child's education is one of the most important financial goals. With rising education costs, starting early and investing wisely is crucial to ensure your child gets the best education without financial strain.

  • Estimating future education costs based on current trends
  • Creating a customized investment plan
  • Selecting appropriate investment instruments
  • Regular review and rebalancing of the portfolio

Retirement Planning

Retirement planning is essential to ensure financial independence during your golden years. A well-planned retirement strategy helps you maintain your lifestyle and meet expenses when you're no longer earning a regular income.

  • Estimating retirement corpus based on your lifestyle
  • Assessing current savings and investments
  • Creating a customized retirement plan
  • Implementing the plan with suitable investment products

Wealth Creation
